About Us

limsward School is a co-education Islamic-based school located in Hill-top Estate Gate, Aboru, Iyana ipaja, Lagos State. We are purpose-driven school who believe in offering holistic education to value-driven society.

We are supported by a highly skilled staff with a wealth of teaching experience and a passion for education, along with dedicated and supportive parents. Together we have established a cohesive team and a determination to prepare our children for life as citizens of our society.

It is our aim at limsward, Insha-Allah, to build strong Islamic foundations which include development of positive Islamic thoughts, good manners and respect and a deep sense of love for Islam as a way of life.
