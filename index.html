Welcome to Limsward International Academy

At Limsward International Academy, we focus on providing an excellent education that integrates both academic knowledge and Islamic values. Our goal is to nurture young minds in an environment that encourages learning, respect, and spiritual growth.

Our school offers a balanced curriculum, with a strong emphasis on Islamic teachings, Arabic, and the Quran. We aim to help each student develop a strong character and a deep understanding of Islamic principles.
